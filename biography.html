My Biography

Samuel Orioye is the owner and founder of GO Wings. We are located on the University of Houston Cafeteria Space. Samuel had a passion of cooking since he was yound, and he decided to pursue it by opening his own restaurant chain.

We have up to 10 locations in Houston. In addition to his restaurant business, Samuel is also a videographer.

Owner's Biography

Oluwatomiwa Orioye is the owner and founder of GO Wings. We are located in the University of Houston Cafeteria Space. Samuel had a passion for cooking since he was young, and he decided to pursue it by opening his own restaurant chain.

We have up to 10 locations in Houston. In addition to his restaurant business, Oluwatomiwa is also involved in cinematography.
